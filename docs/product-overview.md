# Product Overview

## What chefamo is

`chefamo` is a Palermo-first discovery product for families looking for trusted activities for ages `0-14`.

The product is:

- bilingual: `it` and `en`
- city-first: Palermo launches first
- trust-led: source URL, freshness, and CTA matter
- mixed-surface: dated activities plus evergreen places

## Core discovery units

- `activities`: upcoming dated occurrences with a real start and end time
- `places`: museums, libraries, parks, labs, schools, clubs, and family-friendly hubs
- `organizers`: institutions or operators behind programs and places
- `programs`: the canonical listing behind recurring, seasonal, variable, or evergreen offers

## Main user questions

Users should be able to answer three questions quickly:

- What can we do this week?
- Which places are worth knowing even without a fixed slot?
- Who runs this program and can we trust it?

- saved plan for `occurrence` items only

- digest preferences

## Contribution flows

- suggest program
- claim place
- digest signup

## Discovery philosophy

The product is built around one rule:

`Do not fake precision when the source does not support it.`

That means:

- public browsing never depends on auth
- dated discovery only uses real occurrences
- evergreen places can exist without fake schedule rows
- weak social parsing becomes review input, not automatic public truth

## Launch scope

Initial Palermo coverage is centered on:

- culture
- movement
- STEM
- reading
- outdoors

The product is intentionally narrower than a generic events marketplace. It should feel decision-ready, not encyclopedic.
