# English-friendly picks

Some Palermo families need places and programs that can work in English as well as Italian.

This collection is useful for:

- international families settling into the city
- visitors staying longer than a weekend
- local families who want English-friendly staff or instructions

The goal is not to make Chefamo feel generic. It should stay Palermo-first while remaining easy to use for bilingual families.
